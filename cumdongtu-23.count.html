Unit 23: COUNT

Phrasal Verbs Calculation
Count against
Là bất lợi cho ai = be a disadvantage to sb
"For that job her lack of experience may count against her."
Count among
Tính/xếp vào nhóm = include sb/st in a group
"I count her among my closest friends."
Count for
Có giá trị / Quan trọng = be worth st
"Your promise counted for nothing to me."
Count in
Tính cả vào / Bao gồm = include sb/st in an activity
"Count this old tray in with the other things to be sold."
Count on
1. Tin cậy / Dựa vào = rely on sb
"I can count on my parents to help me."
2. Mong đợi / Tính đến = expect st to happen
"I didn't count on being held up in the traffic."
Count out
1. Không tính / Loại ra = not include / eliminate possibility
"Scuba diving? Oh no, count me out!"
2. Đếm từng cái một = count one by one
"She counted the cookies out."
3. Tuyên bố thua (đấm bốc) = counted out (boxing)
"Cooper was counted out in the final round."
