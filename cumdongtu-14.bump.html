Unit 14: BUMP

3 Phrasal Verbs You Need to Know
Tip: Into = Va vào nhau
🤝
Bump into
Tình cờ gặp
= meet accidentally / run into
"We bumped into Amy when we were in town last week."
Tip: Up = Lên cao
📈
Bump up
Tăng lên
= increase
"Prices were bumped up by 10 percent last week."
Tip: Off = Loại bỏ
💀
Bump off
Giết / Thủ tiêu
= kill
"They think someone bumped him off."
