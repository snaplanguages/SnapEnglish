WEST BAY HOTEL - DETAILS OF JOB

  • Newspaper advert for temporary staff
  • Vacancies for (1)
    • 💡 Từ cần điền là danh từ chỉ tên công việc khách sạn đang tuyển. Nữ: "We’re looking for waiters at the moment".
  • Two shifts
  • Can choose your (2) (must be the same each week)
    • 💡 Từ cần điền chỉ thứ có thể chọn. Nữ: "You get one day off and I think you can negotiate which one you want... But it has to be the same one every week".
  • Pay: £5.50 per hour including a (3)
    • 💡 Đặc quyền kèm theo lương. Nữ: "You get £5.50 an hour, and that includes a break".
  • A (4) is provided in the hotel
    • 💡 Nữ: "You can get a meal in the hotel if you want to, and there’s no charge for it".
  • Total weekly pay: £231
  • Dress: a white shirt and (5) trousers (not supplied), a (6) (supplied)
    • 💡 Nữ: "You need to wear a white shirt... and dark trousers... You have to wear a jacket, but the hotel lends you that".
  • Starting date: (7)
    • 💡 Nữ: "Yes, the 28th, in time for the summer" (Nhắc đến June trước đó).
  • Call Jane (8) (service manager) before (9) tomorrow (Tel: 832009)
    • 💡 Tên quản lý: "Her name’s Jane Urwin, that’s U-R-W-I-N". Thời gian: "she usually starts checking the rooms at midday, so before then if you can".
  • She will require a (10)
    • 💡 Nữ: "she will ask you for a reference, so you might like to be thinking about that".
