Unit 15: BURN

Keep the Fire Burning
Away = Biến mất
🕯️
Burn away
Tiếp tục cháy continue burning
"The candles burned away while Molly made a wish."
Bị cháy trụi be destroyed by fire
"All her hair had been burnt away."
Down = Sập/Tắt
🏚️
Burn down
Bị cháy trụi (tòa nhà...) be destroyed by fire
"Their house burned down."
Làm ai phát ngượng embarrass sb
"You just want to burn down everybody to make yourself seem better."
(Ngọn lửa) tắt dần flames become weaker
"The flame burned down and then went out."
Off = Loại bỏ
🏃
Burn off
Loại bỏ bằng cách đốt remove st by burning it
"The plastic is removed by burning it off."
Out = Tắt/Hết
🔌
Burn out
Ngừng cháy / Tắt stop burning
"Before the candles on her cake burn out!"
Ngừng hoạt động (do quá nóng) stop working due to overheating
"The motor in your lawn mower has burned out."
Kiệt sức / Làm việc quá sức be exhausted / overwork
"Traders burn out fast."
Up = Tăng nhiệt/Hết
🌡️
Burn up
Bị sốt cao have a high fever
"You're burning up! she said."
Làm ai tức giận make sb very angry
"The way he treats her really burns me up."
Tiêu hao năng lượng (cơ thể) use energy / calories
"Our body becomes less efficient at burning up calories."
Bị cháy trụi / Phung phí
"Most of the woodland has been burnt up."
