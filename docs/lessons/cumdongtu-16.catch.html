Unit 16: CATCH


Catch of the Day!
★
1. Catch on
Trở nên phổ biến / mốt = become popular
"He invented a new game, but it never really caught on."
Hiểu (sau một lúc) = understand (after a while)
"He didn't catch on at first."
★
2. Catch out
Bắt thóp / Bắt quả tang (làm sai) = discover sb doing wrong
"The examiner will try to catch you out."
Đặt vào thế khó / Bất ngờ = surprise sb / put in difficulty
"Many investors were caught out by the crash."
★
3. Catch up
Đuổi kịp / Bắt kịp (trình độ/tốc độ) = reach the same level/speed
"Go on ahead. I'll catch up with you."
Làm bù (việc đã lỡ) = do st you didn't have time for
"I have a lot of work to catch up on."
Hàn huyên / Cập nhật tin tức = talk to get latest news
"Let's meet for coffee and catch up."
★
4. Catch at
Chộp lấy / Nắm lấy = seize / grab
"A drowning man will catch at a straw."
(Người chết đuối vớ được cọc).
