Unit 27: DIE

Don't be scared, let's learn!
🌬️
Die away
Nhỏ dần rồi tắt hẳn = become gradually weaker/fainter (sound/wind)
"The sound of his footsteps died away."
💡 Tip: Away = Xa dần (Tiếng vọng đi xa rồi mất).
🔥
Die down
Lắng xuống, giảm bớt = become less strong (fire/storm/excitement)
"When the applause died down, he started to speak."
💡 Tip: Down = Xuống (Lửa hạ nhiệt, bão tan).
🍂
Die off
Chết dần chết mòn (từng cá thể) = die one by one / stop existing gradually
"The reindeer herds are dying off."
💡 Tip: Off = Rụng/Rơi (Chết rụng dần như lá).
🦕
Die out
Tuyệt chủng / Mất hẳn = become extinct / disappear completely
"This species has nearly died out."
💡 Tip: Out = Hết sạch (Không còn mống nào).
