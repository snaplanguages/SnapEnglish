LESSON 09: TASK 2 - INTRODUCTION & CONCLUSION

TỔNG QUAN
IELTS Writing Task 2 là phần viết bài luận về một chủ đề nào đó và yêu cầu thí sinh phải phát triển các ý kiến của mình về chủ đề đó. Một số dạng thường gặp bao gồm: Discussion essay, Opinion essay, Causes-Problems-Solutions essay, Advantages-Disadvantages essay.

Mở bài (Introduction) và Kết luận (Conclusion) trong bài luận có vai trò quan trọng đối với tiêu chí Task Response (Mức độ hoàn thành yêu cầu đề). Mở bài giúp người đọc hiểu được vấn đề mà bài sẽ bàn luận và quan điểm của người viết, còn Kết bài tóm gọn lại quan điểm đã nêu.
NỘI DUNG BÀI HỌC
Trong bài học này, ta sẽ tìm hiểu và thực hành:
  • Phân biệt các dạng bài trong IELTS Writing task 2.
  • Cách viết mở bài và kết bài tương ứng với từng dạng bài trong IELTS Writing task 2.

MỘT SỐ DẠNG CÂU HỎI TASK 2 THƯỜNG GẶP

  1. If a product is good and meets customer needs, then people will buy it and advertising is unnecessary. To what extent do you agree or disagree?
  2. Today children spend much time playing computer games and less time on sports. Why is this the case? Is this a positive or negative development?
  3. Some people say that advertising is extremely successful at persuading us to buy things. Other people think that advertising is so common that we no longer pay attention to it. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.

CÁC DẠNG BÀI WRITING TASK 2

Để bài Writing Task 2 được phân thành hai nhóm chính: Hỏi về quan điểm (Opinion-based) và Hỏi về thông tin (Fact-based). Mỗi nhóm gồm các dạng câu hỏi thường gặp khác nhau:

QUESTIONS EXAMPLES
OPINION-BASED Agree or disagree • Scientific research should be carried out and controlled by governments rather than private companies. Do you agree or disagree?
• If a product is good and meets customer needs, then people will buy it and advertising is unnecessary. To what extent do you agree or disagree?
Discuss both views and give your opinions • Some people say that advertising is extremely successful at persuading us to buy things. Other people think that advertising is so common that we no longer pay attention to it. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.
Advantages and Disadvantages • Some students, prefer to take a gap year between high school and university, to work or to travel. Do the advantages of this outweigh the disadvantages?
• Fossil fuels are the main source of energy around the world today. In some countries, the use of alternative sources of energy is replacing fossil fuels. Is this a positive or negative development?
FACT-BASED Causes / Problems / Solutions • Cyclists and car drivers sharing the same road cause some problems. What are the problems? What are the solutions?
• People spend less time at home than they used to. What are the reasons for this change and what are the effects on society and individuals?
Lưu ý: Ngoài các loại câu hỏi trên, một số đề bài Writing Task 2 còn có thể kết hợp cả hai dạng Hỏi về quan điểm (Opinion-based) và Hỏi về thông tin (Fact-based) với nhau. Dạng bài này sẽ được phân tích kĩ hơn ở Bài học 14.

Đề bài: Today children spend much time playing computer games and less time on sports. Why is this the case? Is this a positive or negative development?

Trong đó, câu hỏi Why is this the case? hỏi về nguyên nhân của vấn đề đã nêu, thuộc dạng Fact-based còn câu hỏi Is this a positive or negative development? hỏi về quan điểm cá nhân rằng sự thay đổi này là tích cực hay tiêu cực, thuộc dạng Opinion-based.

MỞ BÀI & KẾT BÀI CHO TỪNG DẠNG CÂU HỎI

I. OPINION-BASED

A. DẠNG ADVANTAGES - DISADVANTAGES

Đây là dạng bài yêu cầu ta đưa ra lợi ích và bất lợi của một vấn đề. Có hai hướng tiếp cận tương ứng với hai dạng câu hỏi như sau:

01. Do you think this is a positive or negative development?

MỞ BÀI
Câu số 01: Giới thiệu chủ đề (TOPIC) bằng cách paraphrase đề bài.
- Cách 1: There has been a trend that (A - paraphrase đề bài)
- Cách 2: An increasing number of people have (A)
- Cách 3: (A - vấn đề trong bài) has increasingly become

Câu số 02: Câu quan điểm (Thesis statement)
- Cách 1: In my opinion, this situation represents a positive/negative development (to + somebody) ...
- Cách 2: In my point of view, this is a beneficial/detrimental development ...

KẾT BÀI
- Cách 1: In conclusion, due to the above reasons, I strongly believe that this phenomenon is a positive/negative development.
- Cách 2: In conclusion, I firmly opine that the above-mentioned situation undoubtedly benefits / does harm to X and Y.
- Cách 3: In conclusion, the overall impacts on X and Y can clearly be seen as a negative/positive development.
Ví dụ:
Đề bài: Advertisement is becoming more and more common in our everyday lives. Is it a positive or negative development?
Mở bài: Advertising has increasingly become an integral part of our lives. In my opinion, this situation represents a negative development to both customers and providers.
Kết bài: In conclusion, the overall impacts on customers and providers can clearly be seen as a negative development.

Gợi ý Mở bài:

Conclusion:
Gợi ý Kết bài:

02. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?

MỞ BÀI
Câu số 01: Giới thiệu chủ đề (TOPIC) bằng cách paraphrase đề bài.
➡ There has been much controversy about whether (A) brings more benefits or drawbacks.

Câu số 02: Câu quan điểm (Thesis statement)
- In my opinion, this trend / situation is more advantageous to [somebody] (because of the aforementioned reasons)
- From my perspective, the drawbacks of this trend clearly surpass/exceed its merits.

KẾT BÀI
- In conclusion, it is apparent that (A - vấn đề) offers more advantages than disadvantages.
Ví dụ:
Đề bài: Advertisement is becoming more and more common in our everyday lives. Do the advantages of this outweigh the disadvantages?
Mở bài: There has been much controversy about whether advertisement brings more benefits or drawbacks. In my opinion, this situation is more advantageous to both customers and providers.
Kết bài: In conclusion, it is apparent that advertisement offers more advantages than disadvantages.

B. DẠNG AGREE-DISAGREE

Dạng bài này yêu cầu ta bàn luận vấn đề và nêu rõ bản thân đồng ý, không đồng ý hay giữ quan điểm trung lập.

01. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

MỞ BÀI
Câu số 01: Giới thiệu chủ đề (TOPIC) đang được bàn luận bằng việc paraphrase đề bài.
- Cách 1: One school of thought holds that (A - ý kiến của đề bài).
- Cách 2: Some people argue that (A - ý kiến 1) while others believe that (B - ý kiến 2).

Câu số 02: Câu quan điểm (Thesis statement)
- Cách 1: Người viết hoàn toàn đồng ý hoặc hoàn toàn không đồng ý với ý kiến của đề bài
➡ From my point of view, I (totally / strongly / completely) oppose/support the statement/former/latter (because ...).
- Cách 2: Người viết đồng ý hoặc không đồng ý một phần (50/50)
➡ Although (A) is justifiable to some extent, I personally lean towards (B).

KẾT BÀI
- Cách 1: (Hoàn toàn đồng ý/không đồng ý) In conclusion, I totally/completely agree/disagree with the idea that ... (due to ...).
- Cách 2: (Đồng ý/không đồng ý một phần) In conclusion, despite the benefits of A, I strongly support that (B) ... because ...

COMPREHENSIVE PRACTICE

I. MATCH INTRODUCTIONS WITH APPROPRIATE QUESTIONS

Hãy điền chữ cái (a, b, c, d, e, f) tương ứng với phần Mở bài (Introduction) phù hợp cho mỗi câu hỏi (Question).

Questions:
  1. Education should be accessible to people of all economic backgrounds. All levels of education, from primary school to tertiary education should be free. To what extent do you agree with this opinion?
  2. Some people believe that children should not be given homework every day, while others believe that they must get homework every day in order to be successful at school. Discuss both sides and give your opinion.
  3. Some educationalists think that international exchange visits will benefit teenagers at school. Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?
  4. Some people think that teachers should be able to ask disruptive children to leave the class. Do you think it is the best way to deal with a disruptive child in the classroom? What other solutions are there?
  5. In some countries, advertisers increase the amount of advertising which tries to persuade children to buy snacks, toys and other goods. Parents object to such pressure on children but some advertisers claim that there is useful information in these advertisements. Discuss both views and give your opinion.
  6. Global warming is one of the most serious issues facing the world today. What are some of the causes of global warming? What can the government and individuals do to solve the problem?
Introductions:
  • It is true that one of the most alarming problems over the globe has always been global warming. There are a number of reasons behind this issue, and several solutions should be taken into account to mitigate it.
  • It is true that some young learners have a short attention span and become disruptive in class. In my opinion, while I agree that excluding the students from the class is a good solution, there are some better ways to tackle this problem.
  • There has been much controversy about whether overseas exchange trips bring more benefits than drawbacks to students in their teens. From my perspective, the drawbacks of this trend clearly surpass its merits.
  • A number of people argue that it is necessary to give students daily assignments after school while others state the contrary. Although both viewpoints are valid, I prefer the former.
  • A number of parents believe that the great volume of advertisements targeting young customers are placing pressure on children while brands argue that their advertising provides useful information. While both viewpoints are valid, I personally prefer the former.
  • One school of thought holds that learners, regardless of their financial resources, should be able to access every level of education from elementary education to colleges and universities without paying tuition fees. In my opinion, I completely agree with this statement.
