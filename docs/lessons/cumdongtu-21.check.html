Unit 21: CHECK

Complete Your List
☑️
Check in
1. Đăng ký (khách sạn/sân bay) (arrive and sign register) "You can save time by checking in online."
2. Báo cáo tình hình (contact to say all is fine) "My son checks in regularly when he's traveling."
3. Check st in: Ký gửi hành lý "We checked in our luggage."
☑️
Check off
Đánh dấu (đã kiểm tra/hoàn thành) (mark something on a list) "Check the names off as guests arrive."
☑️
Check out
1. Trả phòng (khách sạn) (leave hotel after paying) "We're checking out now."
2. Kiểm tra/Điều tra (test/examine facts) "You have to check out the nursing home first."
3. Mượn (sách thư viện) (borrow from official place) "The book has been checked out in your name."
☑️
Check up on
1. Kiểm tra/Giám sát ai (make sure sb is doing right) "My parents are always checking up on me."
2. Kiểm chứng thông tin (find out if st is true) "I need to check up on a few things."
