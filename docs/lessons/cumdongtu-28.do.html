Unit 28: DO

System Operational... Processing Phrasal Verbs
Do away with
Xóa bỏ / Thủ tiêu = abolish / get rid of / kill
> The company did away with free lunches.
Do down
Chỉ trích / Hạ bệ = criticize / humiliate
> Stop doing him down in front of others.
Gian lận (lừa ai) = cheat sb
> That guy did me down.
Do for
Phá hủy / Giết / Làm hỏng = ruin / kill / damage seriously
> The rough roads did for my car.
Dọn dẹp cho ai = do cleaning for sb
Do in
Giết / Thủ tiêu = kill
Làm ai kiệt sức (do sb in) = make sb extremely tired
> The long walk really did me in.
Do out
Dọn dẹp / Trang trí = decorate / clean
> They did the room out with balloons.
Do out of
Ngăn cản / Lừa lấy mất = prevent sb from getting st (by cheating)
> He was done out of his inheritance.
Do over
Làm lại từ đầu = do again
> OK, we'll do it over.
Tấn công / Đánh nhừ tử = attack
> He was done over by a gang.
Trang trí lại = redecorate
Do up
Cài, thắt (dây an toàn, áo) = fasten
> Do up your coat!
Sửa sang / Tân trang = repair / renovate
> We are doing up an old house.
Gói quà = wrap
Do with / without
Do with: Cần / Muốn / Liên quan = need / want / relate to
> I could do with a cup of tea.
Do without: Xoay xở mà không có = manage without
> We'll have to do without a car.
