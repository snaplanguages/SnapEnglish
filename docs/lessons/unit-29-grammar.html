UNIT 29: DAILY SITUATIONS

👋 1. Lời Chào & Cách Đáp Lại

Cách Nói (Greetings) Cách Đáp (Responses)
- Good day/evening, Dr. Olson. How are you? - Good afternoon, ma'am. You're looking well today. - Good morning, sir. - I'm doing very well, thank you. And you? - I'm fine, thank you. - Fine. How are you? - Great, thanks. What about you?
- Hello, May. How're you doing? - Hello. It's a nice evening, isn't it? - Hi, Paul. What are you up to these days? - Morning, Linh. Are you doing okay? - Hey, Jane! What have you been doing these days? - Hey, John. How's it going? - Hi, Kasey. What's happening? - Couldn't be better! Yourself? - Not bad. You? - Okay, I guess. You doing all right? - Can't complain. How about you? - Lousy! - Fantastic!

☎️ 2. Gọi Điện Thoại (Phone Calls)

Người Gọi Điện (Making a call)
Giới thiệu bản thân - Good morning / afternoon / evening. - This is [Your Name]. - Could I speak to ...? Khi người cần gặp đi vắng - Can I leave a message for him/her? - Could you tell him/her that I called, please? - Could you ask him/her to call me back, please? - Okay, thanks. I'll call back later. Kết thúc cuộc gọi - Thank you very much. Have a good day. - Thanks for your help.
Người Nhận (Receiving)
Nhấc máy trả lời - Hello, this is ... How may I help you? - Good morning / afternoon. How may I help you? Xin tên người gọi - May I have your name please? - Who am I speaking with? - May I ask who's calling? Trả lời / Giữ máy / Lời nhắn - Sure, let me check on that / One moment please. - Can I put you on hold for a minute? - Do you mind holding while I check on that? - He's/she's not available / out of the office right now. - Would you like to leave a message? / Can I take a message? Kết thúc cuộc gọi - Is there anything else I can help you with? ... Okay, thanks for calling.

💌 3. Lời Mời (Invitations)

Lời Mời Cách Đáp Lại
- Would you like a cup of coffee? - Would you like to come to my party this Saturday? - Would you care to join us? - Do you want to go out with me tonight? - Do you feel like going for a walk? ĐỒNG Ý - Yes, please. - I'd love to, thanks. - That's very kind/nice of you, thanks. - That sounds lovely, thanks. TỪ CHỐI - I'm sorry to refuse your invitation. - I can't, sorry. I have to work. - Thanks for your invitation but I'm busy now. - I'm afraid I won't be able to come.

🙋 4. Lời Yêu Cầu, Đề Nghị (Requests)

4.1. Hỏi xin phép làm gì

- Can I borrow your books? - Could I have some cake? - Is it OK if I open the window? - Do you mind if I turn on the TV? ĐỒNG Ý - Yes, sure. / Certainly. - No, not at all. (Dùng cho "Do you mind") TỪ CHỐI - Well, I'm afraid... - Sorry, but...

4.2. Yêu cầu ai đó làm gì

- Can you help me with this? - Could you close the door? - Would you mind cleaning the house? ĐỒNG Ý - Yes, of course. - No, of course not. (Dùng cho "Would you mind") TỪ CHỐI - Well, the problem is...

💡 5. Lời Gợi Ý (Suggestions)

Gợi Ý Cách Đáp Lại
- Let's go out for lunch. - How about cooking at home? - Why don't we eat some fruit? - Shall we go by train? ĐỒNG Ý - What a good idea! / Why not? - Sounds good to me. / Yes, let's. TỪ CHỐI - No, let's not. - I don't feel like it.

💡 Thần Chú Mẹo Nhớ - "Cứu Cánh" Giao Tiếp 💡

1. Phép lịch sự với "Mind":
Khi được hỏi "Do you mind / Would you mind" (Bạn có phiền không), nếu bạn muốn đồng ý cho họ làm thì phải trả lời mang nghĩa phủ định: No, not at all (Không, tôi không phiền đâu).
2. Bí kíp đối phó với Lời Khen:
Cách đáp lại lời khen tự nhiên nhất là mỉm cười và nói: "It's very nice of you to say so" (Bạn thật tốt khi nói vậy) hoặc "I'm glad you like it".
3. Sự vi diệu của "You can say that again!":
Câu này có nghĩa lóng thực chất là "Tôi hoàn toàn đồng ý với bạn! Hai tay hai chân tán thành!". Một câu rất hay gặp trong đề thi giao tiếp!
