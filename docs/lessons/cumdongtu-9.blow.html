Unit 9: BLOW

1. Blow down 🏚️
Làm đổ, làm sập, thổi bay (xuống đất)
= knock st to the ground
"A big tree had blown down in the storm."
2. Blow in 🌬️
Bất chợt đến / bước vào
= arrive or enter a place suddenly
"Look who's just blown in!"
3. Blow off 🍃
① Lờ đi, không quan tâm
= ignore
"Just blow off his comments, he's only joking."
② Thổi bay cái gì khỏi bề mặt
= move st off a surface
"That wind today blew off all the clothes."
③ Làm nổ (bom)
= make st explode
"The bomb blasts blew off the side of the building."
4. Blow out 🕯️
① Thổi tắt (nến, lửa)
= extinguish by blowing
"He blew out 60 candles on his cake."
② Làm thất vọng (cho leo cây)
= disappoint sb by not meeting them
"She was supposed to go with me, but she blew me out."
③ Đánh bại dễ dàng
= defeat sb easily
"They've blown out every team this season."
BONUS
Bonus Verbs 🌟
Blow up: Tức giận / Bơm phồng / Nổ tung
"My dad blew up at me." (Nổi giận)
"Blow up the balloon." (Thổi bóng)
Blow over: Qua đi (bão, scandal)
"The storm had blown over." (Bão đã tan)
