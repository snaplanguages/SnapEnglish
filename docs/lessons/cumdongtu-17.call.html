Unit 17: CALL

Please Hold The Line...
📢
Call out
1. Hét lớn / Chỉ trích công khai = shout / criticize publicly 2. Gọi đến (khẩn cấp) = ask sb to come (emergency)
"The governor called out the National Guard."
🎩
Call on/upon
1. Yêu cầu/Đòi hỏi ai làm gì = officially ask sb to do st 2. Ghé thăm / Mời phát biểu = visit / ask to speak
"They called on the company to reverse its decision."
❗
Call for
1. Cần / Xứng đáng = need / deserve 2. Đòi hỏi công khai / Đón ai = demand publicly / pick sb up
"This calls for a celebration!"
📻
Call in
1. Gọi điện (đến đài/chỗ làm) = phone a program / workplace 2. Mời đến / Đòi tiền = ask to come / demand repayment
"Call in to this radio station."
❌
Call off
Hủy bỏ / Dừng tấn công = cancel / stop attacking
"The game was called off due to rain."
🔄
Call back
Gọi lại / Quay lại = phone again / return
"She said she'd call back later."
💻
Call up
1. Gọi điện / Gợi nhớ = phone / remind 2. Gọi nhập ngũ / Mở (trên máy tính) = join army / find on screen
"I called his address up on the computer."
🚉
Call at / Call by
Ghé qua / Dừng lại (tàu xe) = stop at a place / visit briefly
"This train calls at Didcot."
