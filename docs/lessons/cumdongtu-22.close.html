Unit 22: CLOSE

Unlocking the Mysteries of Phrasal Verbs
🏭
Close down
Phá sản / Ngừng hoạt động (kinh doanh) = stop operating as a business
"The local factory closed down in June."
Ngừng phát sóng (Radio/TV) = stop broadcasting at end of day
"BBC 2 closes down at 12:45 tonight."
🦁
Close in (on)
Tiến gần để tấn công / Vây hãm = move nearer to attack
"The lions closed in on their prey."
Bao trùm (đêm tối / thời tiết xấu) = get darker / get worse
"They huddled around the fire as the night closed in."
Ngắn lại (ngày) = days become shorter
"In the autumn the days begin to close in."
🚧
Close st off
Phong tỏa / Cách ly / Ngăn lối vào = stop people from entering
"Police quickly closed off the area."
🤐
Close up
Đóng cửa tạm thời (nghỉ trưa/tối) = shut for a short time
"Why don't we close up and go out for lunch?"
Tiến lại gần nhau (thu hẹp khoảng cách) = come closer together
"Cars were closing up behind each other."
Khép kín / Giấu cảm xúc = hide thoughts or emotions
"She closed up when I asked about her family."
Trở nên hẹp hơn (họng nghẹn lại) = become narrower
"His throat closed up with fear."
🤝
Close with
Kết thúc bằng (tiết mục gì) = conclude st with sb/st
"Let's close with a choir performance."
Thân thiết với ai (tính từ close) = have a friendly relationship
"I was very close with Patty when we were kids."
