Unit 19: CLEAR

Making Phrasal Verbs Crystal Clear
🧹
Clear away
Dọn đi, cất đi (để lấy chỗ) = remove st to leave a space
"I want you to clear all these toys away."
Tránh xa cái gì = move away from st
"Most of the parties cleared away when they heard sirens."
🏃
Clear off
Xéo đi, bỏ đi, biến đi (ra lệnh) = go away/leave
"Clear off or I'll call the police!"
Dọn sạch bề mặt = remove st that is covering an area
"She cleared off the papers on her desk."
📦
Clear out
Rời khỏi nơi nào (thường bị buộc/gấp) = leave a building/place
"The fire alarm cleared the building out in a hurry."
Dọn sạch thứ bên trong = remove contents from a container/room
"We finally cleared out the junk in the attic."
🌤️
Clear up
Trời quang đãng, thời tiết đẹp = become brighter
"I hope it clears up in time for the picnic."
Trở nên khỏe hơn / Chữa khỏi (bệnh) = become better/healthier
"If my cold doesn't clear up, it will ruin my vacation."
Làm sáng tỏ / Giải quyết = make st clear or understandable
"They cleared up the murder mystery."
