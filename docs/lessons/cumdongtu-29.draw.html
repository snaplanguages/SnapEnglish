Unit 29: DRAW

Sketching out the meanings...
Draw aside
Kéo ai sang một bên = pull sb aside (to talk privately)
"The teacher drew Bob aside to have a word with him."
Draw down
Sử dụng hết (nguồn dự trữ) = use up / deplete resources
"We've drawn down our supply of firewood."
Giảm bớt = reduce
Draw in
Thu hút / Lôi kéo vào = attract / involve sb
"They tried to draw in new members."
(Ngày) ngắn lại / Trời tối sớm = become darker earlier
"The days draw in as winter approaches."
Draw on
Sử dụng (kinh nghiệm/nguồn lực) = use a resource/experience
"She drew on her wealth of experience."
Trôi qua (thời gian) = pass slowly
"As the lecture drew on, students dozed off."
Draw out
Kéo dài (thời gian/sự việc) = make st longer
"He drew the meeting out for an hour."
Rút tiền = withdraw money
Động viên ai nói chuyện = encourage sb to talk
"She tried to draw him out."
Draw up
Soạn thảo (văn bản/kế hoạch) = prepare/write a document
"Guidelines have been drawn up."
(Xe cộ) Đỗ lại / Dừng lại = (vehicle) stop
"The cab drew up outside the house."
Kéo lại gần (ghế) = move closer
"He drew up a chair."

276. The government worries about drawing _______ our oil reserves. (Using up)
277. The beginning of this book was so good that it just drew me _______ - I haven't been able to put it down since!
278. To _______ someone's horoscope, you need to know their date and exact time of birth.
279. We do not want to get drawn _______ a long and expensive conflict.
280. The steward drew some wine _______ from the cask.
281. Fund managers drew _______ on cash reserves and bet that the bull market would continue.
282. I drew the nurse _______ so that I could ask a few more questions about my dad's surgery.
283. Depositors drew so much money _______ of the bank that it was forced to close down.
284. The days begin to _______ after the summer solstice. (Get shorter/darker earlier)
285. As the journey was drawn _______ he started to feel tired. (Drag on/Last long)
286. Just when I thought the professor couldn't _______ this lecture any more, he droned on about torts for another half hour.
287. Consumers continue to draw _______ inventories of aluminum, copper, and other metals.
288. Well then, _______ a seat and tell us what you think.
289. She was good at drawing _______ young people and getting them to talk about their fears.
290. He drew _______ his experience as a yachtsman to make a documentary.
Tips:
- UP: Lập kế hoạch (Plan) hoặc Xe dừng (Stop).
- ON: Dựa vào (Rely/Use) hoặc Thời gian trôi qua.
- OUT: Kéo dài (Longer) hoặc Rút tiền.
