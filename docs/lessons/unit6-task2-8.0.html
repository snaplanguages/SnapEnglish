1. Điểm tích cực

Bài viết thể hiện được vốn từ vựng theo chủ đề ở mức khá, với một số cụm từ (collocations) rất tốt như: earn great amounts of money, develop their expertise, connect with like-minded individuals, develop their careers, promoted more easily...

Khi tôi khen ngợi, học viên rất ngạc nhiên vì nghĩ từ vựng của mình quá "cơ bản". Nhưng tôi đã giải thích rằng: Từ vựng có thể không quá cao siêu, nhưng nó truyền đạt ý tưởng một cách hiệu quả và CHÍNH XÁC. Đó là điều quan trọng nhất: Hãy ưu tiên độ chính xác (accuracy) trước khi nghĩ đến sự đa dạng và phức tạp (diversity and complexity).

BÀI MẪU NÂNG CẤP LÊN BAND 8 (My version)

Some people believe that it is better to work for a large company, while others believe that it is better to work for a small company. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

One school of thought holds that working for a large company is more preferable, while many others contend that a small company brings more benefits. Both viewpoints are valid, but I prefer smaller companies.

The claim that people should work for large firms is not entirely baseless. Firstly, these companies often provide stable salaries and bonuses, enabling their employees to attain financial security. During times of economic fluctuation, such as in the pandemic, a well-established company is more likely to guarantee a financial safety net for employees. Secondly, since larger firms typically have a broader range of employees, it is more probable to encounter colleagues who share common interests or career goals. Workers, for example, are more likely to find mentorship and support among colleagues with similar career trajectories.

However, I would advocate for working in smaller companies. In terms of financial security, employees there often perform multiple tasks, making them less dispensable, and deserving of better compensation. This may not be the case for larger corporations, where employees might be seen as mere cogs in big machines, and the competition for promotions can be intense. Moreover, since smaller companies tend to be more laid-back, employees may experience more freedom within their roles. They can also collaborate more closely with their superiors, making their work feel less rigid and more enjoyable. Larger companies, in contrast, often maintain a formal atmosphere and structured hierarchies, which can sometimes lead to a less dynamic and more regimented work environment.

In conclusion, while some people advocate seeking jobs in major corporations, I maintain that employees within these companies are often less appreciated and satisfied with their work. Smaller companies, on the other hand, often offer a more welcoming environment and a greater recognition, making them a potentially more preferable employment option.

[297 words]

TIPS: When dealing with a "discuss both views" question asking you to compare between two subjects (e.g. big companies and small companies), make sure your opinions are clearly stated in the introduction and conclusion.
