Unit 8: BEAT

🎶 The Rhythm of Phrasal Verbs 🎶
☀️
Beat down
1. Nắng to / Mưa to
2. Giảm giá (mặc cả)
(shine brightly / reduce price)
"The sun beat down on the dry fields."
"We beat them down to £95,000."
💡 Tip: Down = Chiếu xuống / Ép giá xuống
🛡️
Beat off
Đẩy lùi / Đánh bại
(succeed in overcoming/preventing)
"The company beat off fierce competition to win the contract."
💡 Tip: Off = Đánh bật ra xa
🥊
Beat (up) on / Beat up
1. Đánh đập liên tục
2. Chỉ trích vô cớ
(hurt sb / criticize unfairly)
"Kids used to beat up on him."
"Stop beating yourself up over this."
💡 Tip: Up = Tác động mạnh lên ai
🔥
Beat out
1. Dập lửa
2. Đánh trống (tạo âm thanh)
(stop fire / make sounds)
"She beat out the flames with a branch."
"Children were beating out on tins."
💡 Tip: Out = Làm tắt lửa / Phát ra tiếng
🕵️
Beat st out of sb
Đánh ai bắt khai ra
(make sb tell st by hitting)
"Police had beaten the confession out of them."
💡 Tip: Out of = Moi thông tin từ ai
