Unit 25: CRY

Phrasal Verbs in the Rain
❌
Cry off
Quyết định không làm điều đã hẹn (Hủy kèo) = decide not to do st arranged
"Luiz cried off at the last moment."
😱
Cry out
La hét (vì đau/sợ) / Gọi to = shout due to fear/pain / shout loudly
"She cried out in pain."
"I heard a voice crying out my name."
📢
Cry out against
Phản đối kịch liệt / Phàn nàn lớn tiếng = complain loudly about st
"People have been crying out against this abuse for years."
🆘
Cry out for
Rất cần / Khao khát = need st very much
"The country is crying out for a change in leadership."
