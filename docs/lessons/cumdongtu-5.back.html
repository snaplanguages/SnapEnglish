Unit 5: BACK

THE PHRASAL VERBS COLLECTION
1. Back away
Lùi lại (vì sợ) / Ngừng ủng hộ
Eg: "She saw the gun and backed away."
Eg: "The government backed away from the tax plan."
💡 Tip: Away = Xa ra (Lùi xa khỏi nguy hiểm/kế hoạch)
2. Back down
Nhượng bộ / Thừa nhận sai
Eg: "Eventually, he backed down and apologized."
💡 Tip: Down = Xuống (Hạ mình xuống, chịu thua)
3. Back off
Ngừng làm phiền / Rút lui (để tránh khó khăn)
Eg: "Back off! There's no need to yell."
💡 Tip: Off = Tắt/Ngừng (Ngừng hành động hung hăng)
4. Back out (of)
Nuốt lời / Rút khỏi thỏa thuận
Eg: "You agreed to come. You can't back out now!"
💡 Tip: Out = Ra ngoài (Rút chân ra khỏi lời hứa)
5. Back up
1. Ủng hộ
2. Lùi xe
3. Sao lưu (dữ liệu)
4. Ùn tắc (giao thông)
Eg: "I'll back you up." (Ủng hộ)
Eg: "Traffic is backing up." (Ùn tắc)
💡 Tip: Up = Lên (Nâng đỡ/Tăng thêm bản sao)

26. The United States had no intention of _______ in its bitter row over farm subsidies.
27. She _______ the menacing look on his face. (Retreated in fear)
28. The government has backed _______ its nuclear weapons strategy. (Stop supporting an idea)
29. Back _______! There's no need to yell at me. (Stop annoying me)
30. She proved her plan was practical, therefore, he had to _______. (Admit he was wrong)
31. They _______ the deal the day before they were due to sign the contract. (Withdrew)
32. The writer doesn't back _______ his opinions with examples. (Support)
33. Back all your files _______ onto a storage device or Cloud. (Copy data)
34. There was an accident, so the traffic was starting to _______ in both directions. (Delay/Queue)
