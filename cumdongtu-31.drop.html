Unit 31: DROP

Falling into Phrasal Verbs
📉
Drop away
Nhỏ dần, yếu dần (về lượng/số) = become weaker/smaller in amount
"Support for him has begun to drop away."
Away = Biến mất dần
🔙
Drop back
Tụt lại phía sau / Bị bỏ lại = move back / drop behind
"He got tired and dropped back as the race went on."
Back = Phía sau
☕
Drop by / round
Ghé thăm, tạt vào thăm = visit informally / short visit
"He dropped by my home on his way from work."
By/Round = Tạt ngang qua
💤
Drop off
1. Ngủ gật (thiu thiu ngủ) = fall asleep quickly 2. Giảm đi (số lượng/giá cả) = become less/lower 3. Thả khách / Trả khách = leave sb/st somewhere
"I dropped off during the play."
"Drop me off at the corner."
🎒
Drop out
Bỏ cuộc / Bỏ học = leave school/activity before finishing
"Too many students drop out of college."
Out = Ra khỏi (trường/cuộc đua)
😱
Drop on
Mang điều khó chịu đến cho ai = deliver st unpleasant to sb
"Sally dropped some bad news on Walter."
On = Đổ lên đầu ai
