Unit 18: CARRY

Pack Your Knowledge & Go
Carry on
Tiếp tục = continue
"You've got to carry on as though nothing happened."
Cư xử không đúng / Dan díu = behave badly / have a sexual relationship
"Is it true that Rachel and Marcus have been carrying on?"
Carry out
Tiến hành / Thực hiện = conduct / perform / complete / fulfill
"The attacks were carried out by nationalists."
Carry off
Thành công (việc khó) / Chiến thắng = succeed / win
"He carried off all the prizes."
Mang đi / Giết chết = remove / cause to die
"He was carried off by pneumonia."
Carry over
Chuyển sang / Kéo dài sang = move to or come from a place/time
"Problems at work carry over into my private life."
Carry through
Hoàn thành = complete
"Determined to carry our plans through to completion."
Giúp vượt qua khó khăn = help to continue
"My savings carried me through that difficult time."
Others
Carry back: Gợi nhớ / Mang trả lại = remind / take back
"The smell carried her back to her childhood."
Carry forward: Cộng vào / Tiến bộ = add on / make progress
